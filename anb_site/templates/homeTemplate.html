Who We Are

We are a non-profit, 501(c)3 rescue group. We have been rescuing and finding homes for unwanted pets since July 2001. We primarily rescue animals that are on death row at local Animal Control agencies to save the lives of the animals. Unfortunately, traditional local shelters are forced to use euthanasia regardless of age, health or breed, as a means to control "overpopulation" of "unwanted" animals. The purpose of our organization is to save discarded companion pets' lives and find new homes for them.
