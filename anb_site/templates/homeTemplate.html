Who We Are

We are a non-profit, 501(c)3 rescue group. We have been rescuing and finding homes for unwanted pets since July 2001. We primarily rescue animals that are on death row at local Animal Control agencies to save the lives of the animals. Unfortunately, traditional local shelters are forced to use euthanasia regardless of age or health, as a means to control "overpopulation" of "unwanted" animals. The purpose of our organization is to save discarded companion pets' lives and find new homes for them.

You Can Help!

We do not have a "public shelter". We have a network of foster homes that help take care of our homeless pets until we can find permanent homes for them. We are in great need of volunteers to help with daytime animal care and weekend adoptions. We need loving foster homes to provide temporary housing for our pets.
